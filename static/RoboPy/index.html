What is RoPE?

RoPE is a project launched to help people become more familiar with the program.
This project is designed to enhance the understanding of programming by assembling robots and programs in a virtual space and actually running them.



Function

・Save and road

You can save your robots and programs, saving you from having    
to recreate them over and over again.
(We use cookie for this system.)





・Pointing out

The block preview makes it easy to see where it will be placed.
The color change of the block indicates the part that is not
connected to the main unit.The error message will show you the
error part of the program.





・Missions

We have prepared a variety of missions. They will help you improve  
your skills.
We are also developing new missions.
